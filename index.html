Channel 03

Submissions

Captured something good on the heliostat? Send it in and it can be added to the site for everyone to see.

Submit a Project

This page doesn't have a live database behind it yet, so submissions are sent by email rather than posted instantly. Fill out the form, hit Prepare Email, and your mail client will open with everything filled in — just attach your image(s) and hit send.

Email attachments don't carry over automatically — this field is just a note to yourself about what to attach before sending.

How it gets on the site

  1. Fill out the form and click Prepare Email — this opens your email client with the details pre-filled, addressed to a.helgerson@ufl.edu.
  2. Attach your photos/data before sending. Original-resolution images work best.
  3. Once reviewed, the project gets added to the public gallery on this page in a future update.
Why not auto-publish? This page is a single static file with no server, so there's nowhere for submissions to be stored automatically. Routing them through email keeps it simple and lets a person check images before they go up publicly.
